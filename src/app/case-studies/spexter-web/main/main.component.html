Pioneering a Unique Market Space: The Spexster Story

RDEVSOL has been instrumental in the development of Spexster, a revolutionary startup creating a bridge between videographers and advertisers.

  • 01 About The Spexter App

    Spexster is a groundbreaking startup designed to bridge the gap between videographers and advertisers. It was born out of the need for filmmakers to market spec videos - footage created for pitches but ultimately not chosen. This marketplace gives advertisers access to pre-existing videos, which can then be modified according to their branding needs, making it a beneficial solution for both parties. During the initial stages of collaboration with the Spexster founders, it was evident that there were no direct competitors, setting Spexster apart from any other business in the market.

  • 02 Requirments

    Our primary task was to shape and define the unique identity of Spexster, focusing on its one-of-a-kind business model. It was crucial to set up an impactful and user-friendly Minimum Viable Product (MVP), factoring in a myriad of creative ideas brought to the table by the Spexster team. The wireframing phase concentrated on optimizing three core pages - the landing page, video list, and detail pages - to maximize engagement and interaction. Further, designing a seamless conversion flow was necessary due to Spexster's unique and high-ticket business model.

  • 03 Our Solution

    Post-finalizing the UX strategy, we proceeded to implement a distinct design scheme. We opted for darker shades to provide the site a cinematic feel, complementing them with lighter backgrounds to ensure comfortable reading of extensive textual sections. Bright accent colors inspired by Spexster's logo were integrated to catch attention and create visual interest. Given the complexity of the site, a custom .NET platform was built from scratch to fulfill all unique requirements and expectations. Interactive elements were coded into the site to encourage user interaction and deepen user engagement.

  • 04 Results

    After thorough testing across multiple devices, we trained the client on the Content Management System and facilitated their independent testing. The absence of prior website analytics, due to Spexster being a new company, allowed us to launch the website at a time that best suited the client. Following the successful launch, we continue to enhance the site's functionality and features, in alignment with the project roadmap. This ongoing development has been well-received by users, indicating a promising future for Spexster in the marketplace.
