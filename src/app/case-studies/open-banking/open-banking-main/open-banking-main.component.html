Redesigning Open Banking: A Case Study

RDEVSOL played a crucial role in the successful transformation of the Open Banking website into a more robust, secure, and user-friendly platform.

  • 01 Identifying the Need

    Open Banking required a more secure and user-friendly website to enhance the digital experience for customers and stakeholders. It was also necessary to ensure seamless integration with data APIs and a platform robust enough to handle data effectively and securely.

  • 02 Our Solution

    RDEVSOL provided a unique solution by completely revamping the website's design and usability. Our development team designed a strategic sitemap to optimize the flow and hierarchy of information. We also ensured secure and robust data handling by setting up enterprise hosting with WP Engine.

  • 03 Outcome

    The outcome was a user-centric, secure, and highly functional website that satisfied Open Banking's needs. The website now efficiently handles large data volumes, and users can interact with the website seamlessly, significantly improving their digital experience.

 

Project Execution Steps

RDEVSOL followed a systematic approach to address the requirements of Open Banking and delivered a solution tailored to their needs. The initial steps included:

  • Identify and understand the main pain points and areas for improvement in the existing Open Banking website.
  • Map out a new UX design to enhance user interaction and ease of navigation.
  • Design a strategic site map to optimize the flow and hierarchy of information.
  • Setup enterprise hosting with WP Engine for robust and secure data handling.

The project execution further involved the development of key components and ensured smooth content migration. These steps included:

  • Configure Google Tag Manager for efficient website analytics tracking.
  • Develop a comprehensive App Store and Regulated Providers Directory.
  • Establish seamless Data API integrations for efficient data interchange.
  • Migrate all necessary content, ensuring no critical data is lost during the transition.
